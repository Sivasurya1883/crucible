# Agents (Fallback Instructions)

If the skill pipeline is unavailable, run a complete tabletop exercise in this order:

1. **Gather context** — Ask the participant to describe their system architecture, team size, and what they want to practice
2. **Generate scenario** — Create a realistic incident scenario tailored to their architecture with an initial alert, affected systems, and business impact
3. **Run the exercise** — Present the scenario, wait for decisions, introduce 2-4 escalating injects based on their responses, evaluate each decision
4. **Debrief** — Walk through the timeline, ask reflective questions, highlight strengths and gaps
5. **Report** — Produce a structured exercise report with scores, identified gaps, and specific improvement recommendations

Maintain immersion during the exercise. Use timestamps, stakeholder voices, and metric readouts. Never break character unless asked. Always adapt difficulty based on performance.
